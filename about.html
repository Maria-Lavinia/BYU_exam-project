ABOUT US

Brand's concept

BYÛ Studio provides customers with a neat and compact box with materials in which customers can use to craft their own jewellery. Once the cast is made, it’s sent back where Byù then uses the mold to create the unique piece with 925 Silver sterling and 14k gold, before the final piece is sent back to the customer.

2019

Bea was inspired by her teachers to start a business where she could deepen her already extensive knowledge. She's been working hard to get things started. She wants to create this brand where individuals are encouraged, invited, included and interested to explore the possibilities of making meaningful jewellery on their own. This is how BYU Studio was created!

2021

Bea studies Jewellery, Technology and Business at KEA - Copenhagen School of Design and Technology. Jewelry design, product development, business, aesthetics, materials, concepts, trends, cultural understanding and innovation are all covered during this course. In other words, Bea learns about the entire process from concept to sold jewelry.

2020

BYU Studio is currently working on a product and has now been able to cast several prototypes made by the focus group in sterling silver. Bea is renovating and polishing them right now to see whether there's any potential there and how can the current setup be improved.

MISSION AND VISION

BYÙ Studio is an inspiring lifestyle brand that puts service over pure product. By this, we move away from meaningless mass production but rather enable our community to engage into the fun and creative process of creating.

BYÙ Studio collaborates with other artists for their boxes / collections. The brand is based on the idea of responsible production and consumption and aims to be transparent in every aspect of the business. Not only do we support the slow fashion principle, but also believe in the power of meaningful and one of a kind jewellery objects.

RESPONSIBILITY

Production

As part of BYÙ’s mission and vision, transparency within every aspect of the business is a main focus for the company. This will be reflected in the way they communicate themselves across all their online platforms, where customers and potential stakeholders will be able to gain insights into the owner and company history, the production of kits and their accompanying materials, as well as the manufacturing and the full casting and molding process of the jewellery pieces.

This will also be the case for the sourcing of materials in use. Access to see their origins will be available to ensure customers and stakeholders that all 3rd parties are properly certified and maintain the same slow fashion principles and values as BYÙ STUDIO themselves.

Consumption

WAX CRAVING TECHNIQUE

While other companies working on a similar concept tend to work with more sculptural types of wax that requires less hand carving with files, BYÙ aims to approach this and make the experience with molding easier, by utilizing a soft wax instead.
