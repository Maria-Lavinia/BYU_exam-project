ABOUT US

Brand's concept

Byù provides customers with a neat and compact box with materials in which customers can use to craft their own jewellery. Once the cast is made, it’s sent back where Byù then uses the mold to create the unique piece with 925 Silver sterling and 14k gold, before the final piece is sent back to the customer.

MISSION AND VISION

Byù is an inspiring lifestyle brand that puts service over pure product. By this, we move away from meaningless mass production but rather enable our community to engage into the fun and creative process of creating.

Byù collaborates with other artists for their boxes / collections. We are based on the idea of responsible production and consumption and aim to be transparent in every aspect of our business. Not only do we support the slow fashion principle, but also believe in the power of meaningful and one of a kind jewellery objects.
